Interesting Facts

  • Nepal is a geographically diverse country that ranges from lowland plains to the Himalayas, including Mount Everest.
  • It has varied climates, from tropical to alpine, influenced by monsoon seasons.
  • Nepal is culturally rich, with many ethnic groups, and is the birthplace of Gautama Buddha.
  • Its economy depends on agriculture, tourism, and remittances from abroad.
  • It is environmentally important but faces risks like earthquakes and climate change.
